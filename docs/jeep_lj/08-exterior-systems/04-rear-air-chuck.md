# 8.4 Rear Air Chuck {#rear-air-chuck}

External air access plate mounted in the tailgate area for tire inflation and air tool use.

/// html | div.product-info

**Type:** Quick-disconnect air chuck plate

**Location:** Tailgate area

**Air Source:** [Air Compressor][air-compressor] via manifold (1/4" air line)

///

## Purpose

- External access to compressed air without opening the vehicle
- Convenient tire inflation at each wheel
- Air tool connection point
- Trail-side air sharing with other vehicles

## Specifications

| Spec           | Value                                    |
| -------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Connector Type | Industrial quick-disconnect (1/4" NPT)   |
| Working Pressure | 150 PSI (matches tank pressure)        |
| Air Line       | 1/4" OD nylon tubing from manifold       |
| Mounting       | Flush-mount plate in tailgate area       |

## Air Line Routing

**Route:** Manifold (under passenger seat) → along interior → to tailgate area

- **Line Type:** 1/4" OD nylon tubing (200 PSI rated)
- **Fittings:** Push-to-connect at manifold, NPT fitting at chuck plate
- **Protection:** Route inside vehicle where possible, protect at penetrations
